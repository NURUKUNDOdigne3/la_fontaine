Education

La Fontaine Community Learning Center a mis en place un programme préscolaire pour permettre à de nombreux enfants de Huye d'apprendre le français dès leur plus jeune âge et ainsi surmonter la barrière linguistique pendant leur scolarité primaire et secondaire. Ce projet est soutenu par la Francophonie, par l'intermédiaire du jumelage Huye et Castres ainsi que par le ministère de l'Enseignement en France.

Culture

We created a space where children and young people could learn and explore diversity through culture, songs, poems, art, and dance, ensuring that they are happy and liberated from past tragedies. Offering art skills has helped children to think for themselves, boost their self-esteem, and improve their academic performance. In this program, young people have learned the importance of teamwork and how it can make a difference when we work together towards a common goal.

Sports

Sports provide a valuable opportunity for people of all ages, genders and backgrounds to come together, breaking down social barriers and fostering inclusivity. When we started , parents were hesitant and worried about safety and differences. However, as the children and youth started playing together, something incredible happened. They formed friendships on the pitch and learned to love one another despite their differences.
