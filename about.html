How La Fontaine Started

Aimable Mugisha and his wife, Belyse, became orphans at an early age. Both Aimable and Belyse were in critical condition. It was a painful childhood: no parents, no siblings, no aunts or uncles, no relatives. There were many questions without answers. The only way forward was education. The government and a charity organization supported Aimable and Belyse in their studies from nursery school through high school. With his commitment and courage, Aimable received a scholarship to continue his university studies in engineering and leadership. After completing his studies in mechanical engineering and leadership courses, especially in community development and football coaching, Aimable and Belyse decided to contribute to the well-being of children, youth, and the community. To express their gratitude and give back,in 2020 they started a nonprofit organization called La Fontaine Community Learning Center. The organization serves the community through three pillars: Sports, Education, and Culture.

La fontaine community center: is an organization that aims to see transformation taking place in the lives of individuals and communities by equipping children and the young generation by creating a supportive environment where individuals can grow, thrive, and contribute to the well-being of others.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Our Mission is to equip children and youth by creating places where to express themselves to become truly actors and creators through Sport-Culture- Education

Our Vision

Our vision is to see the lives of children and youth well equipped and supported where transformed generation help next generation to make the World a better place.

La Fontaine General Sectors

At La Fontaine, we empower our community through diverse and impactful sectors that foster holistic growth and transformation. From promoting physical well-being through sports, to preserving and celebrating our cultural heritage, to nurturing economic resilience through entrepreneurship and business training — each sector is designed to uplift lives and build a brighter, more inclusive future. By addressing the physical, social, and economic dimensions of development, we strive to create opportunities that equip individuals and families with the skills, values, and confidence they need to thrive and lead meaningful lives.
